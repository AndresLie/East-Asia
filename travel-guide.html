Weather Overview

Plan your trip with a quick guide to the seasonal weather in Japan, South Korea, and China.

Japan

Experience all four seasons in Japan. Cherry blossoms bloom in spring, while winters offer beautiful snow in Hokkaido.

South Korea

Winters are chilly but perfect for skiing. Summers are hot and humid, ideal for beach visits in Jeju Island.

China

Diverse climate zones offer everything from snowy mountains in the north to tropical beaches in the south.

Cultural Highlights

Explore the unique cultural experiences these countries have to offer, from ancient traditions to modern events.

Japan

Discover tea ceremonies, sumo wrestling, and the beautiful art of origami.

South Korea

Dive into K-pop culture, traditional hanbok clothing, and the exciting Seoul nightlife.

China

Explore calligraphy, kung fu, and the rich history of the Forbidden City and the Great Wall.
